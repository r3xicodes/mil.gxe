Accessibility Statement

Last reviewed: 2025

mil.gxe is committed to accessibility and strives to make our content usable by everyone, including people with disabilities. We follow best practices such as semantic HTML, meaningful alt text, keyboard navigability, and sufficient color contrast.

What we've implemented

  • Skip-to-content link for keyboard users.
  • Landmark roles (header, main, footer) and semantic headings.
  • Alt text on images and accessible form labels.
  • Responsive layout that works on screen readers and mobile devices.
  • High color contrast and adjustable text sizes via browser settings.

Known limitations

Some legacy pages may have imperfect semantics; we're actively updating them. Video content may not always include captions — contact us if you need an accessible version.

Feedback

If you encounter accessibility barriers, please contact our accessibility team at accessibility@mil.gxe. Provide the page URL, the issue encountered, and any assistive technology you were using.
